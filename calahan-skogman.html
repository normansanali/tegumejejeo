Calahan Skogman, Who Played Matthias in “Shadow and Bone”

Calahan Skogman (age: 29 years; born on May 13, 1993) is an American actor from Green Bay, Wisconsin, who rose to prominence in 2021 after appearing in Eric Heisserer’s action-packed fantasy drama “Shadow and Bone.”

In this highly anticipated Netflix drama, Skogman played the role of Matthias, a formidable warrior from Fjerda who becomes an unlikely ally of Alina Starkov, the show’s protagonist.

But Skogman’s talent extends beyond “Shadow and Bone.” He has also worked on a short film titled “G.I. Jose,” directed by Alejandro Márquez Vela. The film features a talented ensemble cast, including Xochitl Gomez, Stephen Chang, and Elester Latham.

These days, the name of this American gentleman is again floating in the news because the second season of “Shadow and Bone” landed on the OTT giant on March 16, 2023.

For those who want to know more about this rising star, keep reading to discover the fascinating journey of Calahan, from his humble beginnings in Green Bay to his meteoric rise to fame in the entertainment industry.

How Old is Calahan Skogman?

On May 13, 1993, Calahan Skogman (age 29) was born to his father, Wade Skogman, and mother, Stefani, in Green Bay, Wisconsin, America.

Sources revealed that Calahan was a toddler when he and his family moved to Seymour; here, he and his younger brother Clayton spent their entire childhood attending Seymour Community High School.

Not many of you might not know that Cal was a little child when he began playing basketball. His recruitment in the Wisconsin Basketball Coaches Association allowed him to play in the All-State team.

He wanted to become a professional player and ambitioned to join NBA. Following high school, he got accepted into the University of Minnesota Duluth, but after his freshman year, he shifted to the University of Wisconsin–La Crosse.

How Calahan Skogman Became an Actor?

During his time in Wisconsin, he studied broadcasting and even hosted a sports show for WCSports radio. Many of you might be wondering how Calahan changed his mind and became an actor, whereas his dream was to join the NBA.

Well, actually, his mind changed after landing the lead part in the theatre production of “The Metal Heart.” Eventually, he moved to LA, California, and enrolled as an MFA-acting student at the USC School of Dramatic Arts.

A couple of years later, it came out that he landed the part of Matthias Helvar in the TV series “Shadow and Bone.” He won numerous fans with his performance in the show, and oversight became a star. Hopefully, Skogman’s fans will get to watch him in movies soon.
