Marco Hall Net Worth and Profile Summary

Marco Hall Net Worth

Marco Hall has accumulated his wealth through various sources including TikTok, YouTube, and boxing endeavors. He also earns income from commercials, endorsements, and brand partnerships, resulting in an estimated net worth of $3 million.

Marco Hall Biography and Marriage

Reportedly, Marco Hall resides with his wife and children in Warren, Ohio. Aged 37, he received a comprehensive education, completing his elementary and secondary education in his hometown before pursuing higher studies at a reputed university.

Marco Hall Age, Height, and Weight

Marco Hall Career

Marco Hall is a prominent figure in the United States, excelling in boxing and social media. His boxing journey commenced at the age of 26 in 2011, leading him to face notable opponents like Angel Figueroa and Francisco Javier Lopez Chavez. Notably, his career took a leap after training with the renowned Floyd Mayweather Jr. Through his boxing career, he has not only fostered fitness but also mentored several personalities physically and on social media.

In 2019, he ventured into the official TikTok space, propelling his social media standing. His account gained traction, particularly after a video featuring Floyd Mayweather Jr., culminating in over 5 million followers and 100 million likes. Alongside TikTok, Marco is also a well-established YouTube personality with a channel dedicated to fitness and boxing, boasting nearly 80k subscribers as of 2022. He also co-owns the channel “The Beverly Halls” with his wife, amassing nearly 3 million subscribers. Moreover, his official Twitter account has garnered 9.5 million followers and 228 million likes.
