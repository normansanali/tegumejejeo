Where Is Daisy From 'Rock of Love' Now? She Was Accused of Stalking a '90 Day Fiancé' Star

Daisy has been open about her struggles with mental health and addiction. Where is she now?

By Alizabeth Swain

May 15 2024, Published 3:54 p.m. ET

In the glitzy era of reality television that defined the late 2000s, few shows captured the essence of love, drama, and rock 'n' roll quite like VH1's 2007–'09 series Rock of Love. Among the sea of contestants vying for the affection of Poison's lead singer, Bret Michaels, one figure stood out with her unique charm, vibrant personality, and unmistakable style — Daisy de la Hoya.

Known for her emotional openness and roller coaster relationship with Bret during the show's second season, Daisy quickly became a fan favorite, leaving viewers enthralled by her quest for love in the spotlight. Fast-forward to today, the question on everyone's lips is: Where is Daisy from Rock of Love now?

Where is Daisy from 'Rock of Love' now?

After Rock of Love, Daisy ventured back into the entertainment world, including putting out music and even landing her own spinoff show Daisy of Love in 2009. She went on to appear in various projects such as the 2011 Blake Lively movie Hick and the competition series Pageant vs Playmate.

But in the time after Rock of Love, Daisy also encountered a challenging period marked by substance abuse struggles.

In a candid conversation with VH1 in 2016, Daisy shared insights into this tumultuous time, acknowledging her past difficulties but also emphasizing her path to recovery.

"Those things have come to the surface and I’ve been dealing with them as they come," she said. "The good news is that I'm dealing with it, I've dealt with it and I do understand and hopefully you know grown as person and hope I can inspire other people in that way, as well."

In the years since her reality show heyday, Daisy has also gotten involved in animal rights and activism, but she has mostly kept under the radar.

However, she made headlines again in 2024 for a troubling reason.

Daisy from 'Rock of Love' was accused of stalking by a '90 Day Fiancé' star.

Mariah Fineman and Tom Brooks from 90 Day Fiancé have made stalking accusations against Daisy. According to the police report recalled by TMZ, Tom and Mariah claim that Daisy started stalking the two of them in January 2024, in their Los Vegas neighborhood. The report states that Daisy allegedly chased Tom around their neighborhood, invaded his personal space, and sniffed him.

The allegations against Daisy continued to escalate, with Tom claiming that Daisy chased him and Mariah around their apartment building when they went to go get into their car. He claims Daisy jumped into the backseat of their car and didn't leave until police were called. After the police showed up, there weren't any arrests made.

However, TMZ also reports that Daisy insisted these reports aren't accurate. Although she admitted to being in a mental health episode during the time these claims were made against her, she explained that when she claimed her love for Tom, she was actually talking about Tom Cruise.

Daisy acknowledged that she did enter Tom and Mariah's car, a moment that unfolded during her episode — a situation she went on to address with professional support at a treatment facility. While Daisy expressed remorse for her actions, she also shared that she was pained by the fact her mental health challenges were captured on camera.
