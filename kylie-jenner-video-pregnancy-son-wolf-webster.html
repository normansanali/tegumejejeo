Kylie Jenner Shares Glimpse into Her Pregnancy with Baby Wolf

 Posted on August 11, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  358 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Kylie Jenner shared a new heartwarming tribute to her baby son, Wolf Webster.

Today, the beauty mogul posted a video on YouTube that documented her second pregnancy. The montage shows candid reactions to her pregnancy from dad Travis Scott, with whom Jenner shares four-year-old daughter Stormi Webster, and grandma Kris Jenner. Her luxe baby shower, Wolf's nursery, and self-recorded messages from the rest of the Kardashian–Jenner clan also feature in the tribute.

At the end of the video, an audio recording from the delivery room at the time of Wolf's birth is played before the screen fades to read, "To our son / February 2, 2022 / 8lbs 13oz."

Earlier this month, Jenner opened up about struggling with postpartum recovery in a series of videos posted to her Instagram Story.

"I just want to say to my postpartum moms that postpartum has not been easy. It's not been easy, it's very hard," she said. "This experience for me, personally, has been a little harder than with my daughter."

She added that social media can be misleading to some moms. "It's not easy mentally, physically, spiritually, it's just crazy," she continued. "I didn't want to just get back to life without saying that, because I think we can look on the Internet, and for other moms going through it right now, we can go on the Internet and it might look a lot easier for other people and, like, put the pressure on us, but it hasn't been easy for me either, it's been hard."

Jenner added, "Once I realized that ... I was putting some pressure on myself, and I just keep reminding myself I made a whole human—a beautiful, healthy boy. And we have to stop putting pressure on ourselves to be back—not even physically, just mentally—after birth."
