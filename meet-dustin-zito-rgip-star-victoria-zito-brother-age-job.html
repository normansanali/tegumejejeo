Meet Dustin Zito, RGIP Star Victoria Zito Brother

Dustin Zito rose to starlight in 2011 as one of the eight roommates featured on The Real World: Las Vegas. Appearing in the 25th season of the MTV reality series, Victoria’s brother formed a romantic connection with Heather Marter, and the duo continued their relationship out to the real world.

The next year, Dustin and his on-and-off girlfriend Heather then competed in Season 22 of The Challenge: Battle of the Exes. But sadly, they had to leave the series due to an injury Dustin sustained.

Getting back together, the duo then again appeared on Season 3 of the VH1 series, Couples Therapy in 2013. There, they worked out their relationship issues.

Finally, the pair broke up for good after their time on the VH1 series in 2014.

Around the same time, Dustin also allegedly reached under a woman’s dress without consent at a Louisiana bar. He then resisted arrest, resulting in the police officers pepper-spraying and striking him.

Later, Dustin was then charged with felony sexual battery, resisting an officer with violence, and disturbing the peace by public intoxication. 

In 2015, Dustin then returned to the screen (solo) competing in the second edition of The Challenge: Battle of the Exes with another ex, Jessica McCain. Sadly, they were eliminated in the first episode after failing to win the “ex-iled” battles to get back into the competition.  

Thereafter, Dustin retired as a reality star.
