Six-Man Tag Team Match Announced For 2/24 WWE SmackDown

 Posted on August 10, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  208 words  |  Martina Birk

Imperium will be in action on the February 24 episode of .

WWE officially announced that the trio of Intercontinental Champion Gunther, Ludwig Kaiser, and Giovanni Vinci will take on Braun Strowman, Ricochet, and Madcap Moss. Ricochet and Strowman have both unsuccessfully challenged Gunther for the WWE Intercontinental Championship, while Moss earned a shot at the gold by winning a four-way match on the February 17 episode of SmackDown.

The preview, courtesy of WWE’s website, is as follows:

In recent weeks, Braun Strowman & Ricochet failed to unseat Undisputed WWE Tag Team Champion The Usos, and Madcap Moss fell short against Intercontinental Champion Gunther. Now, the explosive trio of Moss, The Monster of All Monsters and The One and Only will look to get back to their winning ways when they join forces against Imperium in a Six-Man Tag Team showdown. Don’t miss all the action of SmackDown, this Friday at 8/7 C on FOX.

The updated card for the February 24 episode of WWE SmackDown is as follows:

  • Rey Mysterio vs. Karrion Kross
  • Charlotte Flair and Rhea Ripley meet face-to-face
  • A special episode of Firefly Fun House
  • Imperium vs. Braun Strowman, Ricochet & Madcap Moss

WrestleZone will have coverage of the show as it airs on Friday.
