Jason Kelce Worried About His Brother And Taylor Swift As Travis Faces Severe Safety Concerns

 Posted on July 16, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  754 words  |  Martina Birk

Highlights

  • Travis Kelce and Taylor Swift's relationship has brought worldwide fame and increased attention to Travis, which has led to safety concerns for the couple.
  • Travis had to move out of his house due to people constantly staying near it and someone even knocking on the back window of his new home.
  • Jason Kelce, Travis' brother, has been concerned about the couple's safety and the overwhelming level of exposure they've received since going public with their relationship.

It's been an incredibly happy few months for Travis Kelce, both professionally and personally. He fell in love and won the Super Bowl, can't get any better than this. Taylor Swift's support has been a very important thing for him since they made their relationship official, and he was moved almost to tears as he hugged her after his big win. The pop star literally traveled halfway across the world (she had been playing four concerts in Japan) to go see him at the Super Bowl, and neither of them are likely to ever forget that day.

Of course, it's not all sunshine and rainbows, but nothing ever is. Travis Kelce's older brother Jason Kelce, who, as much as he adores Taylor, has been outspoken about his concerns for the couple's exposure. He has recently shared that the Kansas City Chiefs player's safety has been put at risk in an unsettling way.

Travis Kelce Was Forced To Move Out Of His Home

It seems dating the greatest pop star in the world has a few disadvantages. As happy as Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce are together, the attention they've been getting since they made their relationship public is not for the faint of heart.

Taylor has been dealing with the highest level of exposure for most of her life, so as annoying as it probably still is, she's used to it. Travis, on the other hand, had a very different level of fame. He was huge among football fans, but now he's been launched into worldwide stardom in a matter of months.

Jason Kelce spoke candidly recently about the struggles his brother has been going through, and some of them are really frightening.

"Travis knows way more than I do, he had to completely move out of his house. People were just staying by his house," he shared. "And the first day he moved into the new house… [in] a gated community, somebody knocks on the back window of the house."

This is something Taylor Swift has sadly been struggling with for years, because as Jason put it, "The Taylor world and the pop culture world, that’s a whole different level."

Jason Kelce Knew This Would Happen When His Brother Started Dating Taylor Swift

Jason Kelce has never kept his concerns about the level of exposure of the couple a secret. Ever since Taylor Swift made her relationship with Travis public, he has been wary of how many people suddenly knew and were interested in his brother.

What happened to Travis Kelce in his house has unfortunately happened to Taylor many times. Recently, one of her stalkers has gotten arrested many times for lurking around her house, and she's had to issue a warning to a man who was tracking her flights.

Knowing all this, Jason has been worried about the couple's (and especially his brother's) safety for a while, as he had the feeling that something like this could happen when he started seeing paparazzi around Travis, and reports of everything he did.

It's a hard line to walk for the older brother. "On one hand, I’m happy for my brother that he seems to be in a relationship that he’s excited about, that he is genuine about. But there’s another end of it where it’s like, 'Man, this is a lot.' There’s paparazzi talking about him fueling his car before the game today and I’m like, 'Is that really necessary information to share?'"

Hopefully, as Taylor and Travis settle into their relationship, the excitement of the new romance will die down, and the power couple will be able to live, if not peaceful, relatively safe lives.
