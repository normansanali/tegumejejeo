Celebs and Their Look-Alike Kids

 Posted on August 31, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  425 words  |  Martina Birk

The apple doesn’t fall far from the tree! Celebrity parents, including Cindy Crawford, Jennifer Garner and Tom Hanks, all share striking similarities — and features — with their kids.

Over the years, fans have pointed out the large number of Hollywood A-Listers whose children are their mini-mes. Kate Hudson, for example, has grown in star power as an actress over the past few decades, but her uncanny resemblance to mom Goldie Hawn is something people can’t stop talking about.

In honor of Mother’s Day 2021, the mother-daughter duo teamed up with La Mer and created a video about their bond.

“I love the idea that I am a mother. That I became a mother and that I had three unbelievably beautiful children. That is a miracle,” Hawn told her daughter during the Instagram clip in April 2021. “I don’t know what I would do without you.”

The Almost Famous star, who was getting a facial from her mom in the clip, replied: “I don’t know what I would do without you mommy! I love you so much.”

Two years prior, Chrissy Teigen noted how much son Miles looks like her husband, John Legend. The couple’s daughter, Luna, however, is a ringer for the Cravings author.

“It’s like someone took my husband, the love of my life, and extended my time with him by giving me his baby years too,” Teigen wrote via Instagram in August 2019 alongside a photo of her little man. “Is that weird? That’s weird.”

In May 2015, Rumer Willis realized that she is also the spitting image of her famous mom, Demi Moore, sharing a “twinning” photo with the G.I. Jane actress via social media.

The St. Elmo’s Fire actress, for her part, exclusively told Us Weekly at the time that she couldn’t be prouder of her eldest child. “I am so proud of her and excited for people to be seeing what I have seen since she was a little girl,” Moore said. “She is incredibly talented, and I love seeing her light shine!”
