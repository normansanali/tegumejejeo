Paddy Considine is an English actor, director, screenwriter, and musician who rose to notoriety in the early 2000s thanks to a series of roles in the British television series The Suspicions of Mr. Whicher (2014). In addition, 24 Hour Party People (2002), My Summer of Love (2004), Cinderella Man (2005), The Bourne Ultimatum (2007), The World’s End (2013), and Macbeth are among his supporting parts (2015).
