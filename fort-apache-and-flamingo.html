Fort Apache And Flamingo Multiple Vehicles Involved In The Crash

 Posted on August 6, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  359 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Fort Apache and Flamingo road saw a horrific accident on Monday, 17th October 2022. 

The Las Vegas Police department is investigating the incident and the crash and whether or not there were any casualties. 

A multi-vehicle collision that happened on Flamingo and Fort is being looked into by traffic authorities who are on the scene. Monday night Apache Road.

The accident in Fort Apache and Flamingo happened on the evening of Monday before 9 P.M.

Metro Police reported that two vehicles that were going down on Flamingo road collided with another car at the intersection.

The Investigation Team Made A Conclusion

In a conversation with Fox5, the Police officials revealed that there might be a possibility that two vehicles might crash into each other or be much more horrible.

The police also revealed that there is a possibility of the involvement of a third vehicle as well at the road intersection.

The officials at the Las Vegas department are still investigating the crash and what might have happened. 

As the investigation followed, the police made a conclusion that the two vehicles collided with a bus stop and two parked cars, with several other things in line. 

A third vehicle was struck in the junction by two eastbound Flamingo vehicles.

The two cars collided at first, then drove eastward, striking a number of stationery items and two parked cars before coming to rest east of the crossing.

As per the police investigation report, no casualties happened. Apart from that, there is no concrete evidence of what happened to the riders or the passengers, if any. 

No significant injuries were reported, according to the police.

The intersection was closed after the crash, and commuters were advised to stay away from the area so that no one could tamper with the evidence, according to LVMPD.
