Alana 'Honey Boo Boo' Thompson Wiki Biography

Alana Thompson, also known as Honey Boo Boo, was born on the 28th August 2005, in Mcintyre, Georgia USA, and is a TV reality star, best known to the world for appearing in such shows as “Toddlers and Tiaras” (2012-2013), and her own show “Here Comes Honey Boo Boo” (2012-2014).

Have you ever wondered how rich Alana Thompson is, as of late 2017? According to authoritative sources, it has been estimated that Thompson’s wealth is as high as $800,000, an amount earned through her successful career in the entertainment industry, active since 2012.

Alana Thompson Net Worth $800,000

Alana Thompson is the daughter of June Shannon and Mike Thompson. Her parents never married and eventually broke up. She has three half-siblings, Anna, Lauryn, and Jessica.

From an early age, Alana was used to getting unhealthy/junk food and soda, which resulted in early overweight problems. In 2012 Alana and her mother became a part of the reality show “Toddlers and Tiaras”, and Alana received massive attention, while her mother was often criticized for the way she was raising young Alana. This included preparing her unhealthy food and giving her Go-Go Juice, which is a mix of Mountain Dew and Red Bull drinks.

Regardless, Alana became extremely popular which resulted in a new reality show “Here Comes Honey Boo Boo”, named after Alana’s nickname, Honey Boo Boo. This earned her a nomination for Teen Choice Award in the category Choice TV: Female Reality/Variety Star. Aside from her own show, Alana subsequently featured in a number of variety shows, such as “Wendy: The Wendy Williams Show” (2013-2014), “The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon” (2014), “The Doctors” (2015), then “Entertainment Tonight” (2015-2017), and in her mother’s own show “Mama June: From Not to Hot” (2017), all of which added to her net worth.

Regarding her personal life, Alana continues to pursue her on-screen career, while not neglecting her education, but she uses every bit of her free time to spend with her family and friends.
