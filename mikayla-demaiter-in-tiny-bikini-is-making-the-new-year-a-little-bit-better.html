Mikayla Demaiter Starts Off 2024 In A Black Bikini

On December 29, 2023, Mikayla shared her last video on Instagram of her on the ice at a hockey rink, leaving many fans to wonder when she was going to post in 2024. She paired the look with a black choker necklace and strappy black heels with thin straps that wrapped around her thin legs several times.

Mikayla Reveals What Heaven’s Dress Code Looks Like

Fans often joke that the popular model looks like an angel, but in one Instagram post, she actually gave fans a glimpse of what the dress code in heaven looked like while standing in the pool in a two-piece bathing suit. Her long blonde hair fell around her face as she smoldered for the shot, accessorizing her look with dangling gold hoop earrings and a hoop necklace.

“No one follows the dress code for heaven better than me,” she wrote in the caption. A geotag placed her in Palm Springs, California. “You took the words out of my mouth on that saying,” one fan commented. “Heaven is a lot better than I thought!” another follower exclaimed. “I would prefer to see you in white as opposed to any other color,” a third fan chimed in while another follower asked, “Do you have a boyfriend?
