The Weeknd, born Abel Tesfaye, has risen to become one of the most influential and successful musical artists of the past decade.

From his humble beginnings to his meteoric rise to fame, The Weeknd's journey is nothing short of extraordinary. In 2024, his net worth stands at a staggering $300 million, solidifying his status as a true megastar in the music industry.

Early Beginnings and Musical Career

Abel Tesfaye's journey to fame began in 2010 when he uploaded a few songs to YouTube, which garnered significant attention. This led to the release of a series of mixtapes compiled into the critically acclaimed album, Trilogy.

Since then, The Weeknd has released multiple chart-topping albums, such as Kiss Land, Beauty Behind the Madness, Starboy, My Dear Melancholy, and After Hours. These projects featured a string of hit singles that have propelled him to global stardom.

  • Philanthropy: Despite his immense fortune, The Weeknd is known for his philanthropic efforts, donating millions to various charitable organizations, including the University of Toronto, the Black Lives Matter movement, and COVID-19 relief efforts.
