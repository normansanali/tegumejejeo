The Career Of Max Verstappen In Racing

Max Verstappen, a Belgian-Dutch racer, is the son of former Formula One driver Jos Verstappen. He participated in the Australian Grand Prix in 2015, becoming the youngest driver in Formula One history. Throughout the 2015 season, he competed for Scuderia Toro Rosso.

Even though he was only eighteen years old at the time, he was promoted to Red Bull Racing as a substitute for Daniil Kvyat later that year.

In December 2021, Verstappen won the Abu Dhabi Grand Prix by passing Lewis Hamilton on the penultimate lap and winning the race.
