WWE Live Event Results From Birmingham, England (11/5/21)

 Posted on August 6, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  134 words  |  Martina Birk

held a live event at the Utilita Arena in Birmingham, England on November 5. The show featured the WWE RAW roster, as the red brand’s champions were in action.

The results are as follows (courtesy of Wrestling Bodyslam):

Damian Priest (c) defeated Kevin Owens, Seth Rollins and Finn Balor

Austin Theory defeated John Morrison

Liv Morgan defeated Carmella

RK-Bro (Randy Orton & Riddle) (c) defeated The Dirty Dawgz (Robert Roode & Dolph Ziggler) and The Street Profits (Montez Ford & Angelo Dawkins)

Reggie (c) defeated Drake Maverick

Becky Lynch (c) defeated Doudrop and Bianca Belair

 Big E (c) defeated Bobby Lashley
